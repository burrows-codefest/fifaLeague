League Rules

Game Sessions

Game sessions are Monday nights between 21:00 and 22:00 unless both players agree to change time and date. If a player is a no show to the arranged time and date then a 3-0 victory is awarded to the opposing team.

League Structure

The first stage is a round robin. Then the top 4 go into the Championship play-off and bottom 2 go into the Cleggor play-off.

Champlin Trophy
Semi 1: 1st vs 4th
Semi 2: 2nd vs 3rd

Final: Semi 1 winner vs Semi 2 winner

Cleggor Spoon
6th vs 7th

Finals are played one lunch time in the Leather Room.

Fifa League Banter Rules

If you get beaten by 5 goals or more you need to post a Facebook apology.
If you get beaten by 10 goals or more you need to send a Moonpig apology.
